What I've Been Up To

HackUTD IX

This past November, I had the amazing opportunity to participate in what is now the 3rd largest hackathon in the United States, with 1,000+ participants and 192 projects submitted! In this event filled 24 hour long hackathon, I teamed up with fellow ACM Officers to create Stellar - a social wellness application dedicated to address today's ongoing mental health crisis. Our hope is to provide users a more personal connection with their friends through "shooting stars" or personalized daily messages/responses that complete a night-sky constellation! I am extremely proud to say that Stellar took 1st place in HackUTD's Beginner Track! This was my first time competing in a hackathon and I'd like to thank the HackUTD officers, ACM UTD and my amazing teammates for such a memorable experience!

Mark Cuban Foundation

Throughout October and November of last year, ACM UTD's Outreach program has partnered with the Mark Cuban Foundation to provide volunteers for their 2022 AI Bootcamp series! Over these past few weeks, I was fortunate enough to be able to mentor high school students as they were introduced to the many aspects of AI like chatbots, computer vision, and natural language processing through Microsoft Azure. During my time volunteering, the other mentors, students, and I got to hear from incredible guest speakers such as Catherine Ordun, David Lee, and Mark Cuban himself!
